display:
    flex  -> the items on this container will take full width
display: 
    inline-flex -> items and width of the container will only take the width of the items

flex-direction: 
    row -> will align items in a row
    column -> will align items in a column
    row-reverse -> will align items in opposite direction
    column-reverse -> will align items in opposite direction

flex-wrap: nowrap -> this will make the items overflow and not stick to the container
flex-wrap: wrap -> this will ajust the items of the container. Items will not overflow

flex-flow -> combination of flex-direction and flex-wrap
    column-reverse
    wrap-reverse

main-axis left to right
cross-axis top to bottom
